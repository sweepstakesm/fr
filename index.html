Get a robot to clean your floors at some of best prices we've seen

Shayna Murphy
Reviewed.com

— Recommendations are independently chosen by Reviewed’s editors. Purchases you make through our links may earn us a commission.

The time for spring cleaning has finally arrived, making for a great opportunity to give your floors a much-needed sweeping. These robot vacuum deals happening at iRobot can help you tackle this chore in a more convenient way than ever before—and for far less.

From now through Saturday, May 9, you can save up to $100 on Roomba robot vacuums and Braava robot mops. Whether you're new to smart home technology or its old hat to you, iRobot's selection of popular WiFi-connected floor cleaners can be a great way to upgrade your home cleaning methods. Here are four of our favorites.

1. Under $500: The ultra-popular i7

Similar to our all-time favorite robot vacuum, the iRobot Roomba i7+ (which is sadly sold out right now), the i7 is a super versatile machine that really does it all—and right now, it's $499.99. It delivers powerful suction and comes with a ton of interesting smart features, like the ability to make virtual maps that can track its cleaning cycles and Alexa and Google Home connectivity. 

Just like the i7+, this model is also compatible with the clean base automatic dirt disposal bin (which you can buy separately, though it's currently sold out), meaning it has the ability to empty itself out after a deep cleaning session. The i7+'s only real advantage to the i7 is that the clean base is included, so you'll have to buy one with the regular model if that's the functionality you really want. All in all, this is a big value considering its usual price tag of $599.99.

Get the iRobot Roomba i7 for $499.99 (Save $100)

2. Save $80: This entry-level WiFi vacuum

Shopping for an entry-level robot vacuum that delivers powerful suction and WiFi connectivity? The Roomba 675 is a solid choice. While we haven't tested it yet, it's similar to the Roomba 690 (out of stock on iRobot's site), one of our favorite affordable robot vacuums. The 675 delivers mid-grade, yet reliable suction—it's not as powerful as the i7, but it's very good compared to most robot vacuums on the market. 

One of the biggest advantages of the Roomba 675 is that it works with Alexa and Google Assistant, so you can use iRobot's custom app to schedule cleanings, monitor activity and even view your vac's cleaning history. During this sales event, you can get it for $80 off its typical list price—its second-lowest price ever after a brief drop to $199.99 during Black Friday.

Get the iRobot Roomba 675 for $219.99 (Save $80)

3. Lowest price ever: The elite s9

Looking for the ultimate robot vacuum? The company's recently released Roomba s9 takes all the great features that are so prized in the i7 line—namely, smart home connectivity and superior suctioning capability—and kicks it up a notch by adding PerfectEdge technology. Specifically, this robot vacuum was made with enhanced, built-in sensors that can scan its surroundings at a rate of 25 times per second, which is faster than any other robot vacuum that the brand offers. Thanks to this, the s9 is optimal for cleaning along wall edges and suctioning up dirt in hard-to-reach corners.

Like the i7, this vacuum can empty itself out via the clean base automatic dirt disposal bin, which is sold separately (but is currently sold out). Right now, you can snag the s9 for its lowest-price ever of $799.99, which is $100 off its normal list price.

Get the iRobot Roomba s9 for $799.99 (Save $100)

4. Under $400: The Braava robot mop

iRobot offers more than just robot vacuums: You can get robot mops, too. Designed to tackle grime, grease and other messes around the kitchen, the Braava Robot Mop with Precision Jet Spray is most closely paralleled by the i-series for iRobot, what with its imprint smart mapping and teaming features, only its purpose is to handle liquid spills. It's compatible with Alexa and Google Voice Assistant and you can schedule it to run when you're not inside, which makes it incredibly convenient. As another perk, it has the ability to "team" up with other Roomba models, like the s-series and i-series robot vacuums, so they can all work in tandem to leave your pad looking extra clean. While you'll almost never see this model go on sale, during this savings event, you can save $100 on it. 

Get the iRobot Braava Jet M6 Robot Mop for $399.99 (Save $100)
